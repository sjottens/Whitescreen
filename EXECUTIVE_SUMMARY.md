## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Thin Content Detection
**Mitigation**: Each page 800-2000 words, unique content, comprehensive FAQs

### Risk: Duplicate Content Across Locales
**Mitigation**: Translation not literal, cultural adaptation, different examples

### Risk: Indexing Delays
**Mitigation**: Timely sitemap submission, proper robots.txt, canonical URLs correct

### Risk: Rankings Don't Improve
**Mitigation**: Monitor monthly, identify content gaps, optimize underperformers

### Risk: User Engagement Low
**Mitigation**: Test CTAs, optimize UX, analyze bounce points, improve tool UX
